Automatically capture isometric images of every part in a SolidWorks assembly and generate an Excel Bill of Materials with embedded thumbnails.

How it works: Point this tool at a SolidWorks assembly file (.sldasm). It will open each part, take an isometric screenshot, and build an Excel spreadsheet with a picture of every component alongside its part number, description, vendor, and quantity.

SolidWorks must be running on this machine before you click Run.

Important: Pack and Go your assembly before running. Files locked in PDM will not open correctly.
